About Our Research Team

Independent research publication — Succession Holding LLC

Succession Holding LLC is an independent real estate investing education resource. We publish research-driven articles on land investing, LLC formation and tax treatment, rental property taxes, depreciation, 1031 exchanges, landlord insurance, property management, and related topics. Our goal is to give investors the frameworks they need to make informed decisions — not to sell them a product or push them toward a particular strategy.

Our Expertise

Our research draws on experience in real estate investing, entity structure and formation, land investment, and rental property management. We have reviewed and analyzed real estate transactions across multiple states, worked with LLC structures in a variety of contexts, and managed both short-term and long-term rental property holdings. That operational experience informs how we write about these topics — not as abstract theory, but as practical realities that investors actually encounter.

Research Methodology

We use publicly available sources to develop our content: IRS forms and instructions, state filing guidance, county assessor records, industry publications, and established legal and tax frameworks. We do not rely on proprietary data or commercial databases. When we cite a tax rule, we reference the actual IRS publication. When we cite a market figure, we identify its source. We believe readers deserve to be able to verify what we publish.

Our editorial review process ensures that all articles are checked for factual accuracy, source citation, and clarity before they are published. Articles covering legal or tax topics are reviewed against current authoritative guidance. We publish with a last-updated date on every article so readers can assess whether the information may have changed.

Our Independence

We are not a registered investment advisor. Nothing published on this website constitutes personalized investment advice or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any real estate or entity interest. We are an educational publication, and our content reflects general research and analysis — not individualized financial planning.

We also want to be clear that we have no financial relationships with any products, services, companies, or platforms that appear on this site. We do not accept payment for reviews, article placement, or endorsements. The topics we cover are chosen because we believe they are important for real estate investors to understand, not because anyone has paid us to write about them.

Not a Registered Investment Advisor

Succession Holding LLC is not a registered investment advisor, broker-dealer, or real estate broker. This website is for educational purposes only. All investment decisions should be made in consultation with a qualified financial advisor, attorney, or tax professional who can assess your individual circumstances.

Contact

For questions about our research, partnership inquiries, or corrections, please reach out via our contact page. We review messages weekly. Please note that we do not accept payment for article placement or product reviews, and such requests will not receive a response.
