About Succession Holding

Built on principles of discipline, transparency, and long-term thinking in real estate investment.

Last updated: April 2026 • By the Succession Holding LLC Research Team

Succession Holding LLC publishes educational research on real estate investing, entity structure, tax planning, and property management. We are an independent real estate investing education resource — not a broker, not a registered investment advisor, and not affiliated with any product or service provider. Our goal is to give investors accurate, well-sourced frameworks for making decisions about real estate.

What We Cover

Our articles address the topics that real estate investors encounter most often in building and managing a property portfolio:

  • Land Investing — evaluating parcels, financing, zoning considerations, and holding strategies
  • LLC Formation and Structure — filing requirements, operating agreements, registered agents, and entity selection
  • Rental Property Taxes — ordinary income treatment, deductible expenses, and reporting requirements
  • Depreciation — residential and commercial depreciation schedules, cost segregation, and IRC Section 179
  • 1031 Exchanges — like-kind exchange rules, identification deadlines, and exchange accommodation titleholders
  • Landlord Insurance — landlord policy components, umbrella coverage, and what standard policies exclude
  • Property Management — tenant screening, lease agreements, maintenance reserves, and eviction procedures
